## My take

This was my first popup blocker for Chrome — shipped in 2012, ran for two years, hit ~250K weekly users before I retired it in late 2014 when I shifted focus to a successor extension. The technique was deliberately simple: intercept `window.open` and the `<a target="_blank">` click path before the page's anti-blocker scripts ran, then flash a tiny in-page notification listing what got blocked so the user could opt back in per-site.
